Who We Are

Real Guides for Real Financial Growth

ProfitTheory was founded in 2021 by a group of finance writers, entrepreneurs, and side hustle veterans tired of the same recycled advice dominating the internet. We wanted something different — content that was specific, honest, and actually tested.

Based in Austin, Texas, our editorial team researches and writes every piece from firsthand experience. Whether it's a guide on landing your first Upwork client or a breakdown of dividend investing strategies, we only publish what we've tried ourselves or verified with industry experts.

Today, ProfitTheory reaches over 95,000 monthly readers across the US, helping them build side incomes, manage money smarter, and work toward the kind of financial independence that makes real life choices possible.
